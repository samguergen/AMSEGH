Member Programs

Every time an older person stops driving, a local businesses runs the risk of losing a customer. ITNLanier offers innovative programs that both fight isolation and keep customers coming through the door of community businesses.

CarTrade™

The CarTrade™ program allows seniors to make use of an unused car and exchange the value of the car for ride credits.

Receive a possible tax deduction or ITN ride credits for yourself or others when you donate or trade your car.

Find out how you can trade you car by contacting us

Gift Certicate

Friends and family can give you a lift by purchasing ITN gift certificates for birthdays, Mother's Day, or any occasion.

A gift certificate for transportation is an original gift that can be used to take fun trips or necessary ones. They are a great way to help your parent or friend fund their Personal Transportation Account™. Celebrate Mother's Day, birthdays, and special holidays with a gift certificate in any amount, sent in a beautiful card by ITNLanier.
Call or e-mail to order.

If you are trying to encourage safe, alternative transportation for your loved one, consider a gift certificate to pay for membership and their first ride. Our trained drivers will transport your loved one with special care -- opening doors, carrying packages, and offering friendly assistance as needed.

Ride & Shop

The Ride & Shop™ program brings paying customers right to the door, then helps them with their packages when they leave.

ITN riders can receive discounts rides at these businesses.

HealthyMiles

HealthyMiles ensures patients can get to your office, whether you’re a doctor, physical therapist, chiropractor or massage therapist.

ITN riders can receive discounted rides to these businesses.

Additional Benefits

    The following programs are additional benefits of membership:
  • Reach Out Program
    Refer a new member or volunteer and receive a $5 gift certificate.
  • Birthday Gift
    Every ITN riding member receives a $5 birthday gift certificate to use towards transportation.

Interested in getting your company or organization involved

It’s easy to sign up for Ride & Shop™ and HealthyMiles, and ITNLanier takes care of all the administrative costs and paperwork.
