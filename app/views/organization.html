Jeff Rock

Chair

Jeff Rock and his wife Nancy have been residents of Hall County for the past five years. Jeff recently retired from Acts Retirement-Life Communities, where he served as the executive director of Lanier Village Estates, a Continuing Care Retirement Community serving six-hundred seniors on the campus located in Gainesville, GA.
